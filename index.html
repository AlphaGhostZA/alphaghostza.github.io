WHO ARE WE?




AF² GES is a private business venture providing for the Sale, Storage and Transportation of Bulk Petroleum Products for the commercial, industrial, domestic and international markets. AF 2 GES is an active independent intermediary of finished fuels with operations that have a global foot- print. Headquartered in Sandton, South Africa, our unique relationship with major petroleum industry leaders enables us to have direct access to refineries in all parts of the world. Our business is the physical trade of finished fuels. Our focus is to create and develop new business opportunities that establish beneficial relationships between buyers and sellers in the petroleum market. We strive for service excellence that exceeds the expectations of our business partners and clients while adhering to all international standards with regard to petroleum products and maritime regulations.

Furthermore, we provide a professional service to the Petrochemical Industry in Health and Safety, commensurate with Dangerous Goods vehicles, Operational Management and Safety within Bulk Storage Terminals, Health and Safety as well as Operational Management of Maritime Installations and Engineering Maintenance Management of Bulk Storage Terminals.

We in addition, also provide a comprehensive service for all other energy related activities and subsequent equipment required. We also have a comprehensive list of subcontractors on our data base that provide services in the IT Sector, Surveillance Sector, Mining Sector(in particular bore hole drilling and diamond mining), Construction companies, Solar Power supplies and installers, Health and Safety experts (in-house and external) that supply, install all safety related equipment.

We are confident that our “can do attitude” experience and knowledge, extensive strategic partnerships, competency and key industry relationships makes us a service provider of excellence.
