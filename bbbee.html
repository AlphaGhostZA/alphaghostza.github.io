>BBBEE

B-BEE is about promoting the achievement of the constitutional right to equality, increase broad- based and effective participation of HDSA’s in the economy and promotes a higher growth rate, increased employment and more equitable income distribution. AF² GES as a proudly South African enterprise is intent on translating this ambition into reality.


The enterprise boasts the following B-BBEE achievements:

Company Ownership

AF² GES is a 100% Black-owned registered proprietary , enterprise number K2020187075.

Management Control

AF² GES is a Black-owned, Black-managed and Black-controlled enterprise. The management team tasked with the daily operations is 100% Black. The Board-of-Directors comprises of three (3) Black male directors, each owning 33,33% of the enterprise.

Skills Development

AF² GES employees are beneficiaries of the Skills Development Plan submitted for a mandatory grant for Workplace Skills implementation to CHIETA on an annual basis.


Corporate Social Investment

Our executive team actively seeks to redress imbalances of the past and will persevere to identify areas of participation in CSI, which include involvement in community development and partnering with our suppliers and clients on developmental programmes.

B-BEE rating

AF² GES qualifies as an Exempted Micro Enterprise and as such is a Level 3 BEE contributor enterprise.

Preferential Procurement

AF² GES makes a concerted effort to procure and secure services from BEE suppliers as part of the enterprises’ efforts towards achieving the national objectives of SMME development.
