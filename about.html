AF² Overview




AF² GES is a B-BBEE company providing design of installations, facilitation of petroleum movement, storage, transportation of petroleum products from dedicated collection locations of our clients to scheduled sites.

The company was formed in 2018 to take advantage of the opportunities presented by the economic transformation in the South African Petroleum and Liquid Fuels Industry. AF² GES sees an opportunity in acquiring a market-share of the Liquid Fuels Sector as identified in the White Paper on Energy, which outlined the government’s key policy objective to achieve “sustainable presence, ownership or control by historically disadvantaged South Africans of approximately a quarter of all facets of the Liquid Fuels Industry, or plans to achieve this”.

Furthermore, AF² GES comprises of formidable black leadership with competencies, experience and knowledge to provide professional services to the petrochemical industry in terms of compliance auditing of Major Hazardous Installations including the ancillary functions such as sale, transport and maritime requirements.

AF 2 GES is an active independent intermediary of finished fuels with operations that have a global foot-print. Headquartered in Sandton, South Africa, our unique relationship with major petroleum industry leaders enables us to have direct access to refineries in all parts of the world. Our sincere dedication to honesty, professionalism, and excellence has propelled our steady growth and enables us to consistently provide our clients with the highest quality products and services. Our core business is the physical trade of finished fuels. Our focus is to create and develop new business opportunities that establish beneficial relationships between buyers and sellers in the petroleum market. We strive for service excellence that exceeds the expectations of our business partners and clients while adhering to all international standards with regard to petroleum products and maritime regulations.

We are confident that our “can do attitude” experience and knowledge, extensive strategic partnerships, competency and key industry relationships makes us a service provider of excellence.
